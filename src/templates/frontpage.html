A sense of wholeness

Acknowledging where we are now, gives us the chance to move forward. How are we feeling most of the day? What is our heart saying when we make some space to really listen?

Having gained distance and sophistication of perception we can turn and recognize who we have been all along, we are our world, knowing itself. If we are able to use this stunning feature of connecting and bonding correctly, we are able to live fully.

If we are in this state to be completely fulfilled we are able to love from a source that is whole, in one piece, we don't have to “use” others to fill our cup. It brings back deep peace and trust that we are truthful, truthful alive. We are able to deeply feel that there are no boundaries between nature and us. It gives us the possibility to truly contain the whole universe.

We are finally able to come “Home” again. We can participate in our world in a richer way, a more responsible and graceful way than before.
